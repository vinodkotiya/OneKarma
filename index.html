Secure Single Sign-On Authentication Platform

OneKarma SSO is an enterprise-grade authentication solution that provides secure single sign-on (SSO) capabilities for organizations. Our application enables users to authenticate once and access multiple enterprise applications seamlessly. OneKarma SSO integrates with Google, AWS Cognito, and other identity providers to deliver secure, scalable authentication for modern businesses.

What is OneKarma SSO?

OneKarma SSO is a secure single sign-on (SSO) authentication application that allows users to log in once and access multiple enterprise applications without repeated authentication.

Key Features:

  • Single Sign-On: Authenticate once, access all your applications
  • Google Integration: Sign in with your Google account
  • Enterprise Security: Built on AWS Cognito for robust security
  • Seamless Access: No need to remember multiple passwords
  • Multi-Platform: Works across web, mobile, and desktop applications

How We Use Your Google Data

OneKarma SSO requests access to your Google account information to provide secure authentication services. Specifically:

  • Basic Profile Information: We access your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account to create and manage your OneKarma SSO account.
  • Authentication Only: We use your Google credentials solely for verifying your identity and enabling single sign-on access to OneKarma.AI applications.
  • No Additional Access: We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, or any other Google services beyond basic profile information needed for authentication.
  • Data Security: Your Google data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties without your explicit consent.
  • You Control Access: You can revoke OneKarma SSO's access to your Google account at any time through your Google account settings.

How OneKarma SSO Works:

OneKarma SSO acts as a centralized authentication service. When you sign in to OneKarma SSO using your Google account, you gain access to all connected applications in the OneKarma.AI ecosystem without needing to log in again. This improves security, reduces password fatigue, and streamlines your workflow.

Our Commitment: We are committed to protecting your privacy and using your data only for the purposes described above. For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.
